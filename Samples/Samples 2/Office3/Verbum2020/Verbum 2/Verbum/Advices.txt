Verbum displays a "Tip of the day" every time you start the program. If you don't want this, just disable the "Show tips on startup" option in this dialog.
You can let Verbum display a "Tip of the day" any time you want by clicking on "Tip of the Day..." in the "Help" menu.
You can set the language for spell checking to either American English or British English, including other languages in the Tools/Spelling Options... dialog.
You can call up the context-sensitive menus by pressing the right mouse button.
If you want more space for your document window, you can switch off all toolbars, rulers and the status bar.
